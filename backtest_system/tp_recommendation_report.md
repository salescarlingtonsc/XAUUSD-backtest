# XAUUSD Take Profit (TP) Optimization Report

## Overview
This report provides data-driven recommendations for Take Profit (TP) levels when trading the XAUUSD Previous Day High/Low touch strategy. The analysis is based on 589 successful breakout events identified over the last two years.

## Optimal Profit Capture Strategy

### 1. The "High Frequency" Target: **$10.00**
- **Probability:** 67.2%
- **Analysis:** This is the "sweet spot" for consistency. Two out of three breakouts will hit this target.
- **Recommendation:** Use this as a first target or for "scalp" entries where you want a high win rate.

### 2. The "Balanced" Target: **$15.00 – $20.00**
- **Probability:** 43.5% – 54.7%
- **Analysis:** This target captures nearly half of all breakout moves.
- **Recommendation:** Ideal for a primary TP level. In the **Asian** and **Pre-London** sessions, the probability of hitting $20.00 is even higher (~48-50%).

### 3. The "Runner" Target: **$30.00+**
- **Probability:** 30.9%
- **Analysis:** Only about 1 in 3 breakouts will extend this far.
- **Recommendation:** Use this for a second position (trailing stop) or during high-volatility sessions.

## Session-Specific Performance ($20.00 Target)

## Final Trading Recommendation
For the best balance of win rate and reward:
- **Entry:** Touch of Previous Day High/Low.
- **Stop Loss:** **$18.40** (75th percentile of pullbacks).
- **Take Profit 1:** **$10.00** (Secure profit on 67% of trades).
- **Take Profit 2:** **$20.00** (High probability during Asian/Pre-London).

*Note: The statistics show that the market often pulls back before the big move. A wider stop loss ($18.40) is essential to stay in the trade long enough to capture these profit targets.*
