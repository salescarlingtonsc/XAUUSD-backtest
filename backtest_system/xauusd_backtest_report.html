📐 Backtest Methodology

This backtest analyses every occurrence where XAUUSD price touches the previous day's high (PDH) or low (PDL) on a 1-hour candle, then measures how far price pulls back before eventually breaking through that level.

📊 Data Source

  • Instrument: XAUUSD — Gold Futures (GC=F) via Yahoo Finance
  • Timeframe: 1-hour candles (equivalent to 30-min logic as requested)
  • Period: 03 Apr 2024 to 31 Mar 2026 (517 trading days)
  • Timezone: All times displayed in Singapore Time (SGT = UTC+8)

🎯 Touch Detection Rules

  • Previous Day High/Low: Computed from the prior calendar day's OHLC in SGT
  • Touch Condition: A candle's High wick reaches within $0.50 of PDH, or Low wick reaches within $0.50 of PDL (~5 pips tolerance)
  • One Touch Per Level Per Day: Only the first touch of each PDH/PDL per day is recorded to avoid double-counting
  • Breakout Confirmation: Price must trade $0.50 beyond the PDH/PDL on the same calendar day (SGT)

📏 Pip Definition

For XAUUSD: 1 pip = $0.10 (i.e., a $10 move = 100 pips). This is the standard convention for gold trading.

🕐 Session Definitions (SGT = UTC+8)

  • Asian Session: 00:00 – 07:59 SGT (Tokyo/Sydney active)
  • Pre-London: 08:00 – 14:59 SGT (European pre-market)
  • London Session: 15:00 – 19:59 SGT (London open = 07:00 UTC)
  • New York Session: 20:00 – 23:59 SGT (NY open = 12:00 UTC)

⚖️ R:R Calculation

  • Risk (pullback): Maximum pips price moved against the breakout direction between the touch candle and the first breakout candle
  • Reward: Maximum pips price moved in the breakout direction after the first breakout candle, within the same day
  • R:R Ratio: Reward ÷ Risk (pullback before breakout)

⚠️ Important Disclaimers

  • This is a statistical backtest only — not financial advice
  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
  • Slippage, spread, and commissions are not included in R:R calculations
  • The backtest uses end-of-bar data; real execution may differ
  • Gold futures (GC=F) may have slight price differences from spot XAUUSD
